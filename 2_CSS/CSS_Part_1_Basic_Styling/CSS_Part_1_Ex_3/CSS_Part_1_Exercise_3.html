Cantus?

A cantus is an activity organised by Belgian, Dutch, French, Baltic, Afrikaans student organisations, and fraternities. A cantus mainly involves singing traditional songs and drinking beer. It is governed by strict traditional rules. (source: Wikipedia)

Cantus latin
Latin Explanation
AD LIBITUM Command means that you have to take a sip of your pint. How big or small that sip is, is up to you.
CANTUS Formalized drinking evening in which songs from the codex are sung.
CODEX Song book used by the cantus-goers. It also contains rules of conduct.
COMMILITONES The members of a student club who do not belong to the praesidium.
CORONA Rectangular arrangement of tables during a cantus.
